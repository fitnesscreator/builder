Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 13, 2025

Welcome to Boss in Pajamas! We know that building your dream website should be as hassle‐free as a well‐made cup of coffee in your favorite PJs. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information while you use our site.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to power our website’s magic (and keep everything running smoothly):

  • Google Account Data: When you sign in using your Google account, we only obtain basic profile details (such as your name, email, and profile picture) for authentication purposes. We do not store sensitive account data on our servers.
  • Google Drive Access: To let you select images from your Google Drive, we request only the minimal permissions necessary. We do not download or permanently store your files without your explicit permission.
  • Usage Information: We automatically collect non-personal data (browser type, device details, and usage patterns) using cookies and analytics tools to optimize your experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to enhance your website-building journey—think of it as adding the perfect splash of color to your design:

  • Authentication & Access: To securely sign you in via Google and allow access to your Google Drive images.
  • Service Delivery: To enable you to create, customize, and download your website with your chosen images and content.
  • Analytics & Improvements: To analyze trends and improve our service, ensuring a top-notch experience.

3. Data Storage and Security

We protect your data as fiercely as you guard the last slice of pizza:

  • No Long-Term Storage: We do not permanently store your Google Drive files or sensitive account information on our servers—everything is managed securely through Google’s APIs.
  • Security Measures: We employ industry-standard security practices to safeguard your data against unauthorized access.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted third-party services to keep things running smoothly:

  • Google Authentication & Drive: All login and file-access operations are conducted securely via Google’s APIs. For more details, please review Google’s Privacy Policy.
  • Analytics: We use analytics tools (like Google Analytics) to monitor website usage and continuously improve our service.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to personalize your experience and understand how you use our site. You can manage cookies through your browser settings, though disabling them may affect functionality.

6. Children's Privacy

This site is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time—just like we keep our nav bar looking fresh. Any changes will be posted on this page along with an updated effective date.
